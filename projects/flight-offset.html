Flight Offset

Flight Offset is a straightforward web application designed to help people estimate their carbon emissions from flying and take action to offset them. The site is hosted at flightoffset.org.

The application provides a simple interface for calculating flight emissions based on departure and arrival locations, flight distance, and passenger class. Users can quickly understand the environmental impact of their air travel in terms of COâ‚‚ emissions.

Currently, the platform integrates with Frontier Climate to facilitate carbon offsetting. Frontier Climate focuses on permanent carbon removal technologies, providing users with a meaningful way to counteract their flight emissions through support of cutting-edge climate solutions.

The project emphasizes simplicity and transparency, making it easy for environmentally conscious travelers to understand and address their carbon footprint from flying. The open-source nature of the project (available on GitHub) allows for community contributions and ensures transparency in the calculation methodology.
